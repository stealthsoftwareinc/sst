:tkgg3ae4_result: R

:tkgg3ae4_url: {cl_sst_copy_cv_url}
include::partials/cl_type_identity_trait.adoc[]

The `{cl_sst_copy_cv}` trait defines `_R_` to be `Dst` overwritten with
the {cl_cv_qualifiers} of `Src`.
In other words, the following steps take place:

. {empty}
Begin with `_R_ = Dst`.

. {empty}
Update `_R_` by removing any {cl_cv_qualifiers}.

. {empty}
Update `_R_` by adding any {cl_cv_qualifiers} from `Src`.
