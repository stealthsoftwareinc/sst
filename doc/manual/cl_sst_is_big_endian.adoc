.{cpp}

  : {ce_std_integral_constant}<bool, /*...*/> {};

----

The `sst::is_big_endian` trait determines whether the byte ordering
of `T` is big endian.

Any {cr_cv_qualifiers} on `T` are ignored.

The `sst::is_big_endian` trait may produce false negatives.
